## What you can build

The TV Quotes API is perfect for:

- Quote-of-the-day apps and widgets
- Discord bots and Slack integrations
- Social media content generators
- Fan websites and tribute pages
- Educational projects and coding tutorials
- Browser extensions and screen savers

## Available shows

The API includes quotes from these popular TV series:

- Breaking Bad
- Friends
- The Office
- Stranger Things
- Brooklyn Nine-Nine
- Parks And Recreation
- How I Met Your Mother
- Seinfeld
- Scrubs
- Modern Family
- And 20+ more!

<Note>
  Show names are case insensitive. You can use "breaking bad", "Breaking Bad", or "BREAKING BAD" interchangeably.
</Note>
